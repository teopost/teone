About me

My name is Ulisse and I was born in March 31 2012 in Dovadola, a small town of 1700 humans located in the hinterlands of Romagna. I should thank Fulvio (Foschi) for that, my breeder.

He was indeed who had introduced my parents to each other and I assure you this shouldn't have been easy since my dad Cat Nap's Jerk-Nils isn't Italian.

Everyone says that I am the right dog and by now I begun to believe it also. My dad Jerk is in fact a true campion of its kind. Also my mom ZigZag is beautiful. It is known: mom is always the mom.

Dovadola is a nice town, but after few months I decided to move a place nearer to the sea. The kennels on the coast though are very expensive, so I moved few kilometers and now I live in Savignano sul Rubicone.

I share my kennel with Teodorani Family, four very nice types. Marinella
